About Abdul Ghafour

Meet Abdo Kafory, a champion from Team KO Squad, the powerhouse in bodybuilding and fitness in the Middle East, and the primary sponsor of world champion Big Ramy. With numerous international championships under his belt, including 1st place at the Masla Menia Championship and 2nd places at Balance Pro and Muscle Fit Championships, Abdo has become a symbol of excellence in the field.

Starting at 50 kilograms, Abdo's journey from a hobbyist to a seasoned professional in bodybuilding spans over 8 years. His transformative path has impacted many, inspiring them to pursue their dreams and focus on health and appearance.

Beyond his online presence, Abdo's influence extends to motivating individuals. The success of the Kafory Classic 2023 championship garnered praise from Dr. Adel Fahim, President of the Egyptian, Arab, and African Bodybuilding Federation. The championship will be supervised by the Egyptian Bodybuilding Federation in 2024 from October.

Abdo's ultimate dream is to become the youngest participant in the Mr. Olympia, the world's premier championship in bodybuilding and classic physique. These achievements reflect his dedication, inspiring countless individuals to strive for greatness.
